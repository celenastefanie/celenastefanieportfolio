CAMPAIGN

The Ottawa RedBlacks

Fall 2015

In order to increase ticket sales for the Ottawa RedBlacks, I designed a campaign to sell a family ticket pack. My concept for this campaign was bringing the family together – Get Closer – and I illustrated this concept in different ways to convey the message. Each image communicates the feeling of unity and togetherness, the same feeling you feel when with your family/team. To appeal to families, imagery and text conformed to a juvenile, cartoonish feel while still conveying the intesity of the sport through bright colours and a bukly header font. Along with print advertisements, web banners, outdoor advertisments and a facebook timeline banner were created.
