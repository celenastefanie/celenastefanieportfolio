Hello!

My name is Celena Dick — middle name Stefanie — and I love to create! I'm currently a third year student from Algonquin College studying Graphic Design. Ever since I was younger I was always doing something artistic whether it be drawing, painting or collaging. With a closet full of magazine scraps and art supplies, I still to this day continue to practice what I love. Apart from art, I'm a full time animal lover with two cats and the memory of the sweetest hedgehog. I will pet any dog that I cross paths with and dog sit in my free time! Music also plays a huge part in inspiring my personal work so feel free to figure out what I was listening to when looking through my projects. Throughout my time at Algonquin, I further developped my skills in Illustration, Typography, Print, UX/UI and a little bit of Web Design!

If you're interested in working together please feel free to contact me or check out my other social media platforms!
