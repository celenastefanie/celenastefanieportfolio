Lemonade

Beyonce Tour Poster

Summer 2016

The Lemonade album by Beyonce deserved a poster that showcased just how ingenius it truely is. Basing my art direction off of the lemonade metaphor, I chose to incorporate a handmade watorcoloured pattern to create a bright and eye catching poster. Keeping true to the essence of the album, the art work is raw, messy in a sense and very hands-on. To contrast this style, I made the layout feel very geometric, keeping basic symmetry and perfect, solid shapes.

The artwork from the poster was later incorporated into a set of three prints for personal use and made to sell.

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Dr P H Martins Ink Watercolours
